This personal project involved the complete rescue and restoration of my grandfather's vintage Piaggio Si, transforming it from a non-running state into a high-performance moped. It served as a practical application of mechanical engineering principles, focusing on internal combustion engine tuning and vehicle dynamics.

### Technical Upgrades
* **Engine Tuning:** Replaced the stock thermal unit with a 65cc cylinder kit to increase volumetric efficiency and power output.
* **Exhaust System:** Installed a custom expansion chamber exhaust to optimize the 2-stroke engine's scavenging process and power band.
* **Mechanical Overhaul:** Performed a complete teardown, replacing bearings, seals, and transmission components to handle the increased mechanical stress, alongside chassis and brake improvements.
