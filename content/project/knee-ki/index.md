Following the laxity assessment tool, I expanded my research to create Knee-Ki, an open-chain kinematic simulator for ex-vivo testing capable of replicating physiological dynamic tasks on cadaveric specimens.

### Project Details
* **Motor Task Simulation:** Implemented a system capable of flexing the knee from 0° to 90°.
* **Actuation & Control:** Developed a custom PID-controlled linear actuator to simulate the quadriceps muscle force during the dynamic movement.
* **Clinical Relevance:** The simulator allows for the direct evaluation of different TKA implant designs under realistic loading conditions, significantly advancing preclinical testing capabilities.
