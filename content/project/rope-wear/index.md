Conducted in collaboration with the University of Florence, this project focused on the tribological characterization and wear analysis of bi-material couplings specifically designed for the nautical sector. 

### Technical Overview
* **Experimental Setup:** Designed and executed testing protocols to simulate operational stresses on bi-material components (e.g. resin, ceramic).
* **Data Collection & Analysis:** Monitored wear rates over time, processing the raw data to identify degradation patterns and failure modes.
