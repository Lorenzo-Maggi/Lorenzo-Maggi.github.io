To combine my passion for cooking with engineering, I co-developed a functional sous-vide immersion circulator (Roner) from scratch. The project required integrating thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and electronic control within a compact, waterproof, and food-safe form factor.

### Engineering Approach
* **Mechanical Design:** Designed the main housing and impeller utilizing CAD software and manufactured the components via FDM 3D printing, ensuring watertight tolerances.
* **Electronics & Control:** Collaborated on the development of the electronic hardware and PID control logic to maintain precise water bath temperatures for extended periods.
* **System Assembly:** Handled the electromechanical integration, waterproofing the heating element, temperature sensors, and circulation pump.
